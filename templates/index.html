SkyTrack AI

GNSS LOS/NLOS & Positioning Tool

Classifies GPS and NAVIC Signals as LOS/NLOS
Combines strong LOS signals to estimate accurate positions.

Input File Format

GPS File: System Time, PRN[9–33], Elev[9–33], Azim[9–33], CNO[9–33], PDOP, HDOP, VDOP

NAVIC File: System Time, PRN[0–8], Elev[0–8], Azim[0–8], CNO[0–8], PDOP, HDOP, VDOP

Note: Ensure time format is YYYY-M-D H:M:S
